Destinations

Paris

Paris, often called the City of Light, is a destination that blends timeless romance with vibrant culture. Strolling along the Seine, you’ll pass historic landmarks like Notre-Dame and the Louvre, while charming cafés line the cobblestone streets, inviting you to pause for a coffee and watch the world go by. From the artistic soul of Montmartre to the elegance of the Champs-Élysées, every corner of Paris feels like a story waiting to be told. And of course, no visit is complete without gazing at the Eiffel Tower, a symbol of dreams, beauty, and the magic that makes Paris unforgettable.



Tokyo

Tokyo is a city where tradition and modernity live side by side in perfect harmony. From the serene gardens and ancient temples of Asakusa to the neon-lit skyscrapers of Shinjuku, every district has its own unique rhythm. You can experience centuries of culture at the Imperial Palace in the morning, shop the latest trends in Harajuku by afternoon, and end the day with sushi so fresh it practically melts in your mouth at Tsukiji. Whether it’s the quiet beauty of cherry blossoms or the futuristic energy of Akihabara, Tokyo is a city that never stops surprising its visitors.
