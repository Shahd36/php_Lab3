Smart Travel Tips

  1. Pack Light, Pack Smart – Stick to versatile clothes you can mix and match, and always roll clothes to save space.
  2. Keep Copies of Important Documents – Have digital and paper copies of your passport, ID, and bookings.
  3. Learn a Few Local Phrases – Even a simple “hello” or “thank you” goes a long way in connecting with locals.
  4. Stay Connected Safely – Get a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi, but avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks.
  5. Use Public Transport – It’s usually cheaper, more authentic, and gives you a feel of local life.
